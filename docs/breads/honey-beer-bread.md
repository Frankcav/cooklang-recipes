# Honey Beer Bread

![Honey Beer Bread](../assets/images/honey-beer-bread.jpg)

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 60 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 12-oz bottle beer
- 2 Tbsp honey
- 4 Tbsp butter

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 loaf pan
- 1 pan
- 1 Spoon

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F.

### Step 2

Grease loaf pan.

### Step 3

Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Stir in beer and melted honey.

### Step 4

Pour half melted butter into pan. Spoon in batter and pour rest of butter on top.

### Step 5

Bake 50 to 60 minutes.
