# Best-Ever Banana Bread

![Best-Ever Banana Bread](../assets/images/best-ever-banana-bread.jpg)

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 60 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 1.25 cups sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1.5 cups banana
- 0.5 cup [buttermilk][1]
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2.5 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup nuts

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 8" loaf pan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine sugar, softened butter, eggs, banana (3-4), buttermilk, vanilla, flour, baking soda, salt, and optional nuts.

### Step 2

Heat oven to 350Â°F.

### Step 3

Bake in 8" loaf pan for 1 hour.
